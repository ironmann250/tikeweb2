PURCHASING POLICY



1. ORDER CONFIRMATION



After placing your order, an order confirmation message appears approving your order as a web top-up and then move to your account, start check-out and pay and your ticket is released in form of an SMS or email depending on your choice.
In case you don’t get that order confirmation, contact our support team at support@tike.rw or call +250787316070



2. SERVICES AND ORDER FEES



Our system is free to use for ticket buyers no additional charges to the ticket net value, just place your order, confirm payment then your ticket is generated.



3. TICKET NUMBER LIMIT



A ticket number limit is a ticket number and usage control tool that allows a given user to buy a limited number of tickets of one event. The maximum number of tickets one user may buy of the same event is 5 tickets in order to avoid ticket fraud and improper usage. One user means one account registered to our website. And for more tickets contact our support team to provide you other options at support@tike.rw or call +250787316070.



4. TICKET TRANSFER



There might be many reasons why our customer may want to transfer his ticket to his friend or neighbor. The original ticket buyer must contact our support team at support@tike.rw or call +250787316070 and get into the procedures and the transfer is done freely.
In that case, the QR code/PIN in the original ticket is canceled hence the ticket loses its validity and a new QR code/PIN is generated to the ticket recipient and the sender gets a confirmation notification approving the transfer success.
The transfer will only occur in a 3 days calendar after the ticket was purchased and not before. For the reselling, the old ticket buyer must wait 7 days before the event day to request a resale.



REFUND POLICY



Our refund system is done in 3 days calendar following the cases listed below in this article. As we do sell tickets on behalf of event organizers, teams, nightclubs, and venues, we are restricted from making a refund to the already bought ticket but, the following are possible cases for the refund. FOR CANCELED OR RESCHEDULED EVENTS When the event is rescheduled, our customers will get a notification informing them about the rescheduling in less than 72 hrs before the predicted event time and inform them about the new dates, hours and venue of the event and those who may want a refund will get it in less than a 3 day calendar (72 hrs) after the predicted event day.



FOR THE CANCELED EVENTS



For the canceled events, our customers are notified in less than 72 hrs before the predicted event day and all our customers who will have bought tickets will get their refund within 3 days following the official cancelation announcement of the event and they all get an email or SMS notification as the refund is made. FOR THE REFUND, THE MONEY ACCOUNT (CREDENTIALS) USED AS PAYMENT MEAN TO THE TICKET PURCHASE IS THE ONE WHICH IS DEBITED AS A REFUND AND WITH NO CHARGES.
This is to mean that if you used the mobile money payment, your mobile money account is the one to be debited in case of the refund or if you used bank cards hence the bank account used is the one to be debited and in case you don’t get a refund, feel free to contact our support team at support@tike.rw or call +25078316070



RESALE POLICY



In case you want to resale or put to AUCTION your ticket, contact our support team at support@tike.rw or call +25078316070 to assist you. Resale will only occur 7 days before the event day and a service fee of 10% is issued to tike.rw and the customer will have rights to sell the ticket on the bid that pleases him/her depending to negotiations between the original ticket buyer and the new ticket buyer and after the agreement the original ticket buyer has to contact tike.rw for the ticket transfer to the new buyer.
OUR RESALE POLICY IS DRIVEN BY UNIVERSAL AUCTION LAWS AND THIS IS TO MEAN THAT THE TIME THE TICKET ON AUCTION ISN’T BOUGHT, THE LOSS IS ON THE ORIGINAL TICKET BUYER.

CANCELATION POLICY

The ticket cancellation must be done by the buyer in less than 7 days before the event day so that his ticket might be put to AUCTION.

The refund is deducted a 10% service fee. The online order, as well as the phone orders, will be canceled if the customer doesn’t make the payment for the requested ticket in less than 30 minutes hence he/she may place a new order.
